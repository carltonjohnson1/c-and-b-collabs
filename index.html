Fun Facts About Us

My Bio

Fun fact, I used to teach myself all kinds of things when I was a kid. If I saw someone on TV doing it I’d have to challenge myself to figure it out.

As a result, I taught myself to juggle, sew, touch type and other seemingly random things at a very early age.

I guess the love for learning, was there even then.

I wanted to be a sports star when I grew up and still hold out hopes that if the number of VEGO chocolate bars you can eat in one sitting ever becomes an Olympic sport, my dream might still become a reality.

Now, my passion is all things website design, development, coding and teaching others.

Barney Bio
